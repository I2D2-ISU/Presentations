Domestic Violence

Domestic abuse rate (per 100,000).

Different denominator might be used to estimate total population (US Census decennial vs ACS 5-year estimates)

Our total number of incidents (numarator) do not match with those in ECI spreadsheet, but is in complete agreement with those reported by Iowa DPS on UCR portal1

  1. Data in Iowa UCR is only available from 2016.

Unemployment Rate

Percent of unemployed persons.

Unadjusted counts

Adjusted counts1

For some years our percentages do not match with those in ECI spreadsheet, but is in complete agreement with those reported by Iowa WorkForce Development on the Local Area Unemployment Statistics tool2

  1. In order to align with ECI’s values, our calculation was changed (now based on unadjusted counts).

  2. Data in LAUS tool are often (retroactively) corrected, which might be a cause for observed differences.

Children in Poverty

Percent of children under six years of age living in poverty (families with incomes below 100% of the U.S. poverty threshold).

Different denominator might be used to estimate total number of children under 6

US Census decennial data1

ACS 5-year estimates

  1. ECI gets the indicator from Kids Count Data Center, instead of getting data directly from the ACS.

Children with Health Coverage by Income Level

Number and percent of minor children with health coverage by income and poverty level1

Poverty level should be explicitly defined (SAHIE reports this data for 6 diffrent levels of poverty: <= 138%, <= 200%, <= 250%, <= 400%, 138% to 400%, and all incomes)2

  1. This is a new indicator, no previous data exist from ECI

  2. Our indicator currently is based on <= 200% of Poverty.

All People in Poverty

  1. This is a new indicator, no previous data exist from ECI

  2. Unlike other similar indicators this one, according to ECI Tool K(B), should be normalized using ACS 5-year estimates.
